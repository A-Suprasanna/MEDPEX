Medicines help prevent, treat, or manage various
health conditions by acting on specific processes
within the body. They include both prescription
and over-the-counter options. Safety relies on
proper dosage, timing, and awareness of possible
side effects or interactions. Regulatory agencies,
like the FDA, ensure medicines are safe and
effective through rigorous testing.
